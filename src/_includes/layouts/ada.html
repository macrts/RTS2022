ADA Information

McCallum Arts Center

Parking

There are five ADA parking spots directly north of the McCallum Arts Center in the lot adjacent to Sunshine Drive.

Seating

In the MAC theater, row K is the only row that is level with the entrance to the theater (meaning no stairs are involved in accessing row K seats). To access any other row, you will have to either go down or go up stairs.

ADA seating is available in the center section of row K. There are six ADA slots, which are empty slots without a physical seat, and six ADA companion seats. Each ADA slot has one companion seat. The ADA slots are marked with an accessible icon on the seat map, and the seat numbers will include a “W” when you purchase your tickets. See the sample map below for the location of the ADA slots and companion seats.

Fine Arts Building

Parking

There are two ADA parking spots in the Fine Arts Building parking lot directly south of the McCallum Arts Center. They are at the back of the lot in the southwest corner, closest to the Fine Arts Building.

Seating

The FAB theater seating is currently being reconfigured, but ADA seating is available. Please contact the MacTheatre box office at boxoffice@macrts.org to arrange for ADA seating.
