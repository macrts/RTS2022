(and Alumni Parents)

One of the most powerful ways we can support MacTheatre students past, present, and future is building and maintaining an alumni network. Whether you’re a high-schooler visiting prospective schools or navigating college auditions, a recent graduate headed off to college, or a college graduate ready to break into the performing arts (or any other industry) professionally, being able to reach out to others with the shared experience of MacTheatre is invaluable.

Feeling nostalgic?

If you want to take a trip down memory lane, you can check out the playbills from every MacTheatre season going back to 2015/2016. There are also performance photos going back to 2010

If you have playbills or photos that you’d like to share from earlier shows, please send them to us at president@macrts.org so we can post them. We’d love to have them for our archives.
