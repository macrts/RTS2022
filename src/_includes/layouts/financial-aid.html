MacTheatre and the Royale Theatre Society believe that all students, regardless of their financial circumstances, should have access to high quality theatre productions and programs. No student will be prevented from participating in theatre because of an inability to pay. For any questions regarding RTS financial aid applications, please contact scholarships@macrts.org.

Production Fee Assistance

Production Fees (also known as Fair Share Fees) are annual fees that are required of all performance theatre majors, technical theatre majors, musical certificate students, and any other student participating in a production. Learn More.

If you would like to request financial assistance to cover the cost of your production fees, please click the button below. Applications are kept confidential and are seen only by the RTS Scholarships Committee chair.

Texas Thespians Festival Trip Fee Assistance

Each year, MacTheatre students have the option of attending the Texas Thespians State Festival, a state-wide conference hosting over 8,000 middle school and high school theatre students for three days of educational seminars, workshops, performances, and competitions. Learn more.

If you would like to request financial assistance to cover part or all of the cost of your Texas Thespians State Festival trip fees, please click the button below. Applications are kept confidential and are seen only by the RTS Scholarships Committee chair.
