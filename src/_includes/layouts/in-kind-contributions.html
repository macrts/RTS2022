Contributor Benefits

When the value of your contributions totals $50 or more for a single show, you will receive the benefits below:

  • Your logo with a link to your website will be included on the RTS home page and supporters page for the remainder of the season.
  • Your logo will be highlighted in the post-show email sent to all of the patrons of the show that the contribution applied to.

When the value of your contributions totals $500 or more in a single school year, you will be acknowledged as an associate producer and receive the related season sponsorship package during the following school year.

When the value of your contributions totals $1000 or more in a single school year, you will be acknowledged as a lead producer and receive the related during the following school year.
